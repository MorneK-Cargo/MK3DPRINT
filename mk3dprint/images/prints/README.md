# Featured Prints Images

This directory contains images for the "What We Print" featured section on the home page.

## Required Images

Place the following images in this directory:

1. **mechanical-designs.jpg**
   - Description: Gears, brackets, hinges, and functional mechanical parts
   - Recommended: 800x600px minimum, high-quality photo of 3D printed mechanical parts
   - Examples: Gears, brackets, custom hinges, precision components

2. **miniatures.jpg**
   - Description: Detailed figurines and tabletop gaming pieces
   - Recommended: 800x600px minimum, close-up photo of detailed miniatures
   - Examples: Fantasy figures, gaming pieces, collectible figurines, detailed characters

3. **household-items.jpg**
   - Description: Organizers, holders, desk accessories, and storage solutions
   - Recommended: 800x600px minimum, product-style photo of functional household items
   - Examples: Phone stands, cable organizers, desk organizers, drawer dividers

4. **prototypes.jpg**
   - Description: Engineering prototypes and product development models
   - Recommended: 800x600px minimum, photo of technical/engineering prototypes
   - Examples: Robot parts, product mockups, engineering models, test pieces

5. **art-fashion.jpg**
   - Description: Creative sculptures, artistic designs, and wearable pieces
   - Recommended: 800x600px minimum, artistic/creative photo
   - Examples: Sculptures, artistic vases, jewelry, wearable art pieces

6. **replacement-parts.jpg**
   - Description: Custom parts for repairs, replacements, and upgrades
   - Recommended: 800x600px minimum, product photo of custom replacement parts
   - Examples: Replacement knobs, custom clips, adapter parts, upgrade components

## Image Specifications

- **Format:** JPG (JPEG) preferred for optimization
- **Size:** Minimum 800x600px (will be displayed at 240px height)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 4:3 or landscape preferred
- **Quality:** High-resolution, well-lit photos of actual 3D printed items
- **Optimization:** Compress images to ~100-150KB for web performance

## Styling Notes

- Images will be displayed in 240px height cards
- Images will zoom slightly on hover for visual feedback
- Cards have a subtle teal border and shadow effect
- Text overlays appear below images, so clear foreground is preferred
